06

How It Works

See → Act → Speak → Repeat

See

Claude Vision assesses the scene in under 20 tokens. Book open, closed, done? Content page or blank? The robot decides what to do next.

Act

Separately trained motor policies — open, turn, close — each learned from demonstration. Precise, gentle, repeatable.

Speak

Text streamed to ElevenLabs before the page is fully processed. Sub-second latency. Voice cloning makes it personal.
